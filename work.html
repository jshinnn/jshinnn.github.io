Home Work Play

Mushroom House in Blender: Designed and built the geometry from scratch for a photorealistic 3D scene of a whimsical mushroom house, focusing on ray tracing, lighting, UV mapping and texturing, depth of field, and volumetrics. Rendered in Blender.

Nationwide Water Contamination Analysis: Worked with the NRDC to analyze PFAS contamination in U.S. drinking water. Used Python, NumPy, pandas, ArcGIS Pro, and Tableau to formulate actionable insights from complex geospatial analyses.

Splunk: Developed an end-to-end mobile push notification system, enabling real-time data alerts. Worked on backend integrations, user interface components, and server deployment using REST APIs.

U.S. National Parks: Collaborated with The Washington Post graphics team to rebuild and redesign an interactive piece documenting every national park in the U.S. Used React for dynamic rendering and HTML/CSS for responsive design.

Police Shootings Database: Upgraded the framework and tools (Django, Python3) used to power a database that The Washington Post uses to track all fatal police shootings in the U.S. since 2015.

2021 Tokyo Olympics Coverage: Enhanced accessibility for the Tokyo Olympics medals tracker and schedule tables by implementing ARIA roles and labels, ensuring compatibility with screen readers. Received over 1 million unique visits.

Daikon Kids Go Home: Designed and developed a narrative-driven video game in Godot, following a sentient daikon radish's journey to escape a supermarket. Leveraged physics-based mechanics and hand-drawn assets to create an engaging gameplay experience (tldr; daikon experiences many life crises -- don't we all?).

Rotten Tomatoes Sentiment Analysis: Worked with a friend to analyze the biases, tone, and sentiment of movie reviews written by movie critics and published on Rotten Tomatoes, using d3.js, Python, and pandas. Check out our visualizations.

NLP Research: Fine-tuned a LLaMA-3-8B model using Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) to mitigate harmful behaviors in a sleeper agent. Results demonstrated DPO's ability to reduce undesirable behaviors without prior knowledge of specific misaligned actions. Read about our study.

Research with Stanford CS + Psychology: Studied how causal structures influence explanatory reasoning. Co-authored a research paper and implemented experiments in jsPsych. Recruited and managed participants via Prolific; all code is open-source on GitHub.
